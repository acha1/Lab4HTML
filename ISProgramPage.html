Bachelor of Science in Information Systems Degree
The BS program prepares students for all potential career positions in the IS field. These include: Systems Analysis and Design, Computer Networking and Health Care Informatics. For successful completion of the degree, all students must satisfy the general education requirements that are explained in the UMBC Undergraduate Catalog. Effective Fall 2013, all Information Systems majors must complete at least 9 upper level IS classes (numbered >= 300) taught by the IS department at UMBC.
